Professional Organizations

IEEE Computer Society (IEEE CS)

A global organization supporting computing professionals through conferences, publications, and technical standards. Members gain access to research libraries, workshops, and communities across AI, software engineering, and data systems. Website: computer.org

Association for Computing Machinery (ACM)

The world’s largest computing society, known for its Digital Library, SIG groups (such as SIGKDD and SIGCHI), and influential conferences including KDD, FAccT, and CHI. Website: acm.org

Government Organizations

NIST — Information Technology Laboratory (ITL)

A U.S. federal research lab that produces standards and guidance for cybersecurity, AI safety, privacy, and risk management. Key outputs include SP 800-53 and the NIST AI RMF 1.0. Website: nist.gov/itl

National Science Foundation (NSF)

A federal agency funding foundational research in computing, machine learning, and data science. The NSF supports academic research labs and publishes open-access project outcomes across scientific disciplines. Website: nsf.gov

Academic Journals

Journal of Machine Learning Research (JMLR)

A leading open-access journal covering theoretical and applied machine learning. Known for its rigorous peer review and strong emphasis on reproducible research. ISSN: 1533-7928

ACM Transactions on Intelligent Systems and Technology (TIST)

A well-regarded academic journal publishing research on AI, knowledge systems, NLP, recommender systems, and intelligent interfaces, with a balance of theoretical and application-focused work. ISSN: 2157-6904
