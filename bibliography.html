Professional Organizations

IEEE Computer Society (IEEE CS)

A global organization supporting computing professionals through standards development, publications, and education. Student and professional members gain access to the IEEE CS Digital Library, learning tools, and conferences such as ICDE and ICDM. Website: computer.org

Government Organizations

NIST — Information Technology Laboratory (ITL)

A U.S. federal research laboratory that develops standards and guidance for cybersecurity, privacy, and AI — including the NIST AI RMF 1.0 and SP 800-series documentation. Website: nist.gov/itl

Academic Journals

Journal of Machine Learning Research (JMLR)

A leading open-access journal covering foundational and applied machine learning research, published continuously with LaTeX submission requirements. ISSN: 1533-7928
